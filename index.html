Web Compliance Exception Form - WebA11y Tracker - Instructions

Please contact OPSAccessibility@ontario.ca to reqest an alternative format or for assistance in completing this form.

Important

Please read the Guide to Determining Web Compliance Exceptions on InsideOPS before completing this form.

Disclaimer
This form was designed to help ministries document exceptions to the web accessibility requirements in section 14 of the Integrated Accessibility Standards Regulation (O. Reg. 191/11) (IASR) under the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 and should be used to inform the annual accessibility attestation process; specifically the application of exceptions. This form and the associated guide reflect legal input and guidance from OPS subject matter experts but are not intended to provide or substitute for legal advice. Documenting an exception through this form does not necessarily ensure compliance with the IASR. Documented exceptions should be supported by legal advice where necessary and appropriate.

Please note that the OPS Accessibility Office will be receiving a copy of this form for data collection and analysis.
